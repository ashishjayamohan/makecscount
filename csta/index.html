CSTA FAQ

How do I join a CSTA?

CSTA Website

How do I find a chapter to join?

See map above or CSTA's chapter page

I'm not really a CS teacher- can I still join?

Yes! The only requirement for CSTA membership is being interested in CS education.

Can I attend another chapter's meetings?

Most chapter meetings are open to anyone. Reach out to the leader.

Do I have to pay?

No. Basic membership is free. If you want to upgrade to CSTA+, you can pay $50/yr.

Can I start my own CSTA chapter?

Of course. Central Coast and Oakland/East Bay are in progress.
